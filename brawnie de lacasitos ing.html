    Lawnitos brawnie with cookies marias

    ingredients


    225 g of butter
    225 g of 70% dark chocolate or your favorite chocolate
    150 g of wheat flour
    150 g of sugar
    150 ml. of liquid cream or milk cream (35% M.G.)
    2 eggs
    4 tablespoons whole milk
    1 tablespoon of vanilla essence
    200 g of dark chocolate (coverage)
    3 tablespoons of cream (coverage)
    100 g of butter (coverage)
    8 cookies (the ones you like best)
    Lacasitos to decorate our Brownie
      Preparation of brownies with Lacasitos
    • We greased a pastry tray. The one I have is 30 x 30 cm, the thickness of the brownie will depend on the dimensions of the tray.
    • Spread with butter and sprinkle with flour.
    • Put in a bowl the grated chocolate or in small pieces with the cream or milk cream and the butter in the microwave for 3 minutes at low temperature, if necessary more time, we make short intervals of time so that we do not burn the chocolate.
    • This form is the fastest, although we can melt the chocolate in the water bath.
    • We sift the flour in a bowl and add the sugar. Pour the hot chocolate mixture on top.
    • We beat well until we get a homogeneous paste and when we mix, we incorporate the eggs, a few drops of vanilla essence and 4 tablespoons of whole milk.
    • In a bowl, break Maria's cookies into large pieces (in this case, 8 cookies).
    • You can use any type of cookie, this time I replaced the nuts with the pieces of cookies.
    • Pour the mixture into the tray and insert the pieces of biscuit. We bake the brownie in the preheated oven at 190º C for 25-30 minutes (as I mentioned in some other recipe, it all depends on the oven, always look with a fork that stays dry).
    • We let it cool and when it is cold we cover it with the chocolate of the cover and we put it for a little while in the fridge.
    • To make the cover prepare in a bowl the chocolate, the liquid cream and the butter in the micro for 5 minutes at low temperature and let it cool.
    • We apply the cover with a spatula in the sponge cake when it is cold, we decorate with the lacasitos and we put it in the fridge so that there is a layer of hard chocolate. We let our chocolate brownies and Lacasitos cool down before unmolding. Cut into pieces and serve.
