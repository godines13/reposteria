nutella cheesecake cake

ingredients


Dark chocolate (for the first layer) ... 150 g
Liquid whipping cream (for the first layer) ... 200 ml
Milk (for the first layer) ... 200 ml
Powdered curd on (for the first layer) ... 1
Milk chocolate (for the second layer) ... 150 g
Liquid whipping cream (for the second layer) ... 200 ml
Milk (for the second layer) ... 200 ml
Powdered curd on (for the second layer) ... 1
White chocolate (for the third layer) ... 150 g
Liquid whipping cream (for the third layer) ... 200 ml
Milk (for the third layer) ... 200 ml
Powdered curd on (for the third layer) ... 1
Milk (for the third layer) ... 200 ml
Mary-type cookies (for the base) ... 200 g
Butter (for the base) ... 100 g
    Preparation
  • We will start preparing the base, for this we crush with a kitchen robot or by rolling the cookies inside a plastic bag.

  • When they are in fine powder we add the melted butter and form a dough that we will stick to the base of a 20 cm removable cake mold lined with baking paper on the bottom. We booked in the fridge.

  • We will continue preparing the layer of dark chocolate. We put a little scoop with the cream and 100 milliliters of milk to be heated, leaving the other 100 aside and in which we will dissolve the curd envelope carefully watching that no lumps remain.

  • Once the milk and cream are hot add the dark chocolate in small pieces and stir with rods until it dissolves well in the liquid. Add the rest of the milk with the dissolved curd and bring it to the boil, stirring the mixture for two minutes more or less making sure it does not stick to the bottom of the cup. It is important to boil so that the curd works and the cream is solid.

  • Pour the black chocolate cream over the base and let it rest so that it cools while we prepare the chocolate milk.

  • To prepare the chocolate milk cream we follow the same instructions as for the previous one, the only thing that changes is the type of chocolate. Once cooked this cream pour over the black chocolate pouring carefully over a spatula so that we do not bore the layer of dark chocolate. Let it rest again while we prepare the layer of white chocolate.

  • We did the same process as the other two times, but with the white chocolate, and we ended up throwing the layer of white chocolate as the final layer. We leave a minimum of six hours in the fridge to set perfectly and carefully remove molds once cool.
