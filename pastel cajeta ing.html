Cajeta cake


ingredients

Cake:

5 eggs

1 cup of sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup of evaporated milk (250 ml)
1 cup of oil (250 ml)
1 ½ cups of hot cakes flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 ½ cups of cajeta

Three Milks Mix:
1 ½ cups of cajeta

1 can of half cream
1 can of evaporated milk
30 ml of brandy
Oven preheated to 180ºC.
Preparation:
For the cake, blend the eggs, sugar, vanilla, evaporated milk, oil, hot cakes flour and baking powder. Pour this mixture into a cake mold previously greased and floured. Bake for 1 hour or until a toothpick comes out clean. Remove from the oven let cool and unmold. For the mixture of three milks, beat with the help of a balloon whisk all the ingredients to integrate. Reservation. Chop the cake with a fork and bathe with the mixture of three milk, let it completely absorb the milk and cover it with the remaining cajeta. Refrigerate for 2 hours and offer.

PREPARATION TIME: 25 minutes

BAKING TIME: 1 hour
