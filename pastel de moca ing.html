Moka Cake


ingredients

CAKE

1 1/2 cups of hot water

1 tablespoon instant coffee granules NESCAFÉ TASTER'S CHOICE 100% Pure Instant Coffee Granules

1 cup of coffee powder NESTLÉ COFFEE-MATE Powdered Coffee Creamer

2 1/3 cups of general-purpose flour

1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

1 1/3 cups (8 oz.) Of high quality white chips NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE® Premier White Morsels

1/3 cup of vegetable oil

1 2/3 cups of granulated sugar

4 large eggs

2/3 cup (5 fl. Oz. Can) of evaporated milk NESTLÉ CARNATION Evaporated Milk

2 1/3 tablespoons white vinegar

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2/3 cup cocoa powder NESTLÉ TOLL HOUSE Baking Cocoa

COVERAGE

2/3 cup of high quality white chips NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE® Premier White Morsels

1/3 cup of butter or margarine

1 tablespoon instant coffee granules NESCAFÉ TASTER'S CHOICE 100% Pure Instant Coffee Granules

1 1/2 teaspoons of water

2 packages (3 oz. Each) of cream cheese

4 to 4 1/2 cups of powdered sugar

TO PREPARE THE CAKE:

COMBINE Taster's Choice water and granules in a medium bowl. Incorporate the coffee cream using a rod whisk. Combine 1 2/3 cup of flour and baking soda in another medium bowl. Microwave 1 1/3 cup of the chips and vegetable oil into a large uncovered bowl suitable for such use at MID-HIGH power (70%) for 1 minute; REVIVE THEM. It may be that they do not completely lose their shape. If necessary, cook them in the microwave for 10 to 15 minutes several more times until they melt. Add the coffee mixture, sugar, eggs, evaporated milk, vinegar and vanilla extract to the melted chips using a wire whisk. Gradually add the flour mixture until it is well combined. (The shake will be thin.) Pour 3 1/4 cups of smoothie into a medium bowl; Incorporates the rest of the flour. Pour the smoothie into the molds that you prepared.

INCORPORATE the cocoa into the rest of the shake, combining it well with a rod whisk. Slowly pour even amounts of the cocoa shake into the center of each of the molds. (The cocoa shake will spread evenly to the sides.)

BAKE the shake for 40 to 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow the molds to cool for 10 minutes on metal grids; desmóldalos and leave them in the metal grid to finish cooling. Spread the cover between the layers of the cake, on top and on the sides.

TO PREPARE COVERAGE:

Microwave 2/3 cup of the pieces and butter in a large uncovered bowl suitable for such use at MID-HIGH power (70%) for 1 minute; REVIVE THEM. It may be that they do not completely lose their shape. If necessary, cook them in the microwave for 10 to 15 minutes several more times until they melt. Combine Taster's Choice coffee granules and water in a small bowl. Add the cream cheese and the coffee mixture with the melted chunks. Gradually add the powdered sugar by beating the mixture until you obtain a consistency that can be spread. Makes approximately 2 1/2 cups.
