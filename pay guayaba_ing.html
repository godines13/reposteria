pay of guava


ingredients

450 grams of guavas cut in half and without seeds

450 milliliters of cream

2 packages of cream cheese

1 can of condensed milk

1 ½ teaspoon of vanilla essence

4 tablespoons of grenetine

½ cup of cold water

1 ½ packet of crushed maría cookies

90 grams of melted butter

10 guavas cut in half and without seeds

1 striped piloncillo

4 tablespoons of grenetine

½ cup of water
preparation

COMBINE the crushed cookies with the butter until you have a wet and sandy mixture; spread it in a cake mold. COOK the guavas, including the 10 pieces for the ate, in boiling water for 35 minutes. LICUATE the guavas (minus 10 for the ate) pass them through a sieve and return the puree to a clean blender jar. HYDRATE the grenetine in the cold water, let stand for 10 minutes; Melt it in the microwave for 15 seconds until there are no lumps left. ADD the cream cheese, milk, cream and vanilla essence to the blender. ADD the melted grenetina to the blender in the form of thread and with the engine on. POUR the guava filling to the mold on the biscuit base; Refrigerate for at least two hours. HEAT the guava puree in a pot, add the grated piloncillo; cook for 15 minutes over medium heat, stirring constantly. HYDRATE the grenetine in the cold water, let stand for 10 minutes; Melt it in the microwave and add it to the pot. REMOVE from the heat and cool for 30 minutes. POUR the mixture on the guayaba pie and refrigerate for two hours.
