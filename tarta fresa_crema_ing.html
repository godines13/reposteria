strawberry cake with vanilla cream

ingredients


100 grams of Flour

60 grams of Butter
1 pinch of Salt
200 grams of Milk
3 units of egg yolks
22 grams of corn starch
150 grams of brown sugar
1 pinch of Vanilla Essence
150 grams of Strawberries
Steps
  • Place the flour and a little salt in a large bowl, add the cold and chopped butter in squares and with the help of a knife we begin to chop until we get the texture of a sand, without very complicated with the knife we can do it with the fingertips very carefully without heating the butter.
  • Add a little water to integrate the dough until it is well compacted and homogeneous.
  • We cover a mold for cakes to which we have previously added butter and flour, we make some punches in the dough with a fork so that we do not inflate and bake for 25 minutes at 160ºc or until this gilded
  • To continue with the preparation of the cake we make the pastry cream placing the yolks together with the sugar and corn starch and beat well until the mixture is very creamy.
  • We heat the milk with the essence of vanilla and when it is boiling add a little to the yolks and mix, then pour the yolks in the rest of the milk and mix vigorously until it thickens, if they are lumps in the mixture we can pass it through a sieve.
  • Place the pastry cream on the cake and decorate to taste with all strawberries.
