nutella cheesecake cake

ingredients


2 cups of chocolate cookie for the base
1/2 cup of melted butter, for the base
2 tablespoons nutella or hazelnut cream, for the base
3 pieces of cream cheese at room temperature
1 1/2 cups nutella or hazelnut cream
2 envelopes of hydrated and melted gelatin
1/2 cup of bitter chocolate for coverage
1/2 cup of milk chocolate for coverage
1/4 cup of whipping cream for coverage
1/4 cup of butter for coverage
    Preparation
  • In a bowl mix the chocolate cookies with the melted butter and Nutella. Form the base of the cheescake into a cake pan and flatten until completely uniform. Refrigerate 10 minutes. Reservation.
  • In a bowl, add the cream cheese with the Nutella, pour the melted grenetine in the form of a thread, mix and cover the base of the biscuit.
  • Refrigerate 2 hours or until set.
  • Cencourage a pot with water and place a bowl, add the ingredients of the cover and mix until completely incorporated. Chill lightly and pour the cover over the cheescake. Refrigerate 30 minutes more, decorate with strawberries. Cut and enjoy.
