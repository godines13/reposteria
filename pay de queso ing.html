Cheese pie


ingredients

1 cookie package Maria 170 g

1/2 cup melted butter

1 can of condensed milk

1 can of evaporated milk

3 pieces of egg

1 bar of cream cheese 190g

preparation

Preheat oven to 160 ° C.

Grind the cookies, reserve a bit of the cookie to cover the pie and leave a golden crust.

Mix the rest of the cookies with the butter to form a paste and place in the base of the pie pan. Reservation
.
Blend the remaining ingredients. Empty the mixture into the mold and sprinkle the cookie that you reserved over.
Bake in a bain-marie and cover with foil for 1 hour or until firm.

Cool and refrigerate 2 hours before serving.
