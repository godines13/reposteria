chocolate cake

ingredients
Chocolate cake
Spray oil

1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

2 ½ cups flour

2 cups of sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

3 large eggs at room temperature

¾ cup of canola oil

½ ek yogurt cup

2 tablespoons of vanilla extract

Bitumen
1 package of cream cheese at room temperature

Half a cup of unsalted butter at room temperature

3 cups of sugar glass

½ cocoa powder cup

¼ of tablespoon of salt
Preparation

1. Preheats the oven to 180 °. Cover with two 20 cm non-stick molds with the spray oil.

2. Mix the cocoa with 1 ½ cups of boiling water in a medium bowl until it is integrated. Reservation.

3. Mix the flour with the sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowls. Add the eggs, oil, yogurt and vanilla and mix in the blender at medium speed for 1 minute. Reduce the speed and add the cocoa mixture until everything is combined. Finish joining with a spatula.

4. Divide the mixture between the two molds. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean. For approximately 30 to 40 minutes.

5. Remove the molds from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes.

6. With a knife remove the edges of the cake and turn the biscuits onto a plate, let cool completely.

Bitumen
1.Mix the cheese and butter in the blender at high speed.

2. Add sugar, salt and sifted cocoa and mix at low temperature.

3. Add the vanilla extract and temrina to mix with a spatula.

Mounting
1.Add a bit of polish on the center of the plate on which you will serve the cake.

2.Place a sponge cake over this portion of bitumen. Cover the bread with more bitumen.

3.Place the second layer of cake and cover with bitumen.

Recommendation
You can cover with berries to give a flavor contrast between sweet and sour.
