brawnie of 2 chocolates

ingredients


Butter...120 g

White chocolate...150 g

Dark chocolate...75 g

Peeled and roasted hazelnuts...75 g

Egg...L2

Brown cane sugar...120 g

Pastry flour...120 g


We preheat the oven to 180º with heat up and down. We greased or lined with baking paper a mold, preferably square 18 cm side. Melt the butter with 25 g of white chocolate and 25 g of dark chocolate, the rest of the chocolate is chopped and reserved.

Meanwhile, we beat the eggs with the sugar until they mount and the mixture is whitish. We add the mixture of chocolate and melted butter in the form of yarn when it is almost cold and continue beating. We incorporate the sifted flour with the impeller, and chocolates and hazelnuts chopped. Pour the dough into the tray and smooth the surface.

We bake for about 20-30 minutes. We will watch the time so that the brownie does not become too much, otherwise it would lose all its juiciness. We take out of the oven, let it cool slightly and cut the portions before serving.
